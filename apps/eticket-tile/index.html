E-ticket tile

A Quick Settings tile for easy e-ticket scanning

Bored of your device's screen being to dim and constantly rotating when scanning your e-tickets ? This Quick Settings tile for Android 6 and up is made for you: It will let you lock the screen rotation and ramp up the brightness in an instant, and then restore your screen rotation setting and favourite brightness once the scanning process is over.

Other than the tile itself, you will find a comprehensive guide to enable it in a flash.
